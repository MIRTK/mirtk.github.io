Detailed Description

Base class for external surface force terms

Subclasses implement in particular external forces for deformable surface models such as inflation/balloon forces and intensity edge forces. In case of a tetrahedral mesh, these forces only apply to the corresponding triangular surface of the simplicial complex boundary.
