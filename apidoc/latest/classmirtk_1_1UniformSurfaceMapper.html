Detailed Description

Piecewise linear surface mapper with uniform convex combination weights

  • Tutte (1963). How to draw a graph. Proc. London Math. Soc, 8(May 1962), 743767.
  • Taubin (1995). A Signal Processing Approach To Fair Surface Design. SIGGRAPH, 351358.
  • Floater (1997). Parametrization and smooth approximation of surface triangulations. Computer Aided Geometric Design, 14(3), 231250.
