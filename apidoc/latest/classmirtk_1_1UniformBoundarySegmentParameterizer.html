Detailed Description

Boundary curve parameterization with uniform distance of curve points

This boundary curve parameterization is referred to as uniform parameterization in Floater (1997).

  • Floater (1997). Parametrization and smooth approximation of surface triangulations. Computer Aided Geometric Design, 14(3), 231–250.
